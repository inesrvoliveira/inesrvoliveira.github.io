What personal data we collect and why we collect it

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Analytics

Our website and applications collect anonymised analytic data which we use to help us understand where our users come from and improve content to be more targeted to our users.

Crash Reports

Our applications collect anonymised data about the state the application was in when it crashed. It does not include any personal data in this collection.

Ad data

In our applications, we use Google Adsense to show ads. Google track users using an “advertising Id”, we don’t personally use this for anything, however, Google may use it to serve you with more personalised ads.

Who we share your data with

Our analytics, crash reports and ads are using other companies platforms for collection and analysis and is subject to their own privacy policies. Otherwise, we do not share any data with anyone else.

How long we retain your data

The anonymised data is kept indefinitely so that we can track trends over time.
